Help Page

How to Bid on Jobs

To bid on a job, follow these steps:

  1. Find a job listing you are interested in.
  2. Read the job description and requirements carefully.
  3. Click on the "Place Bid" button associated with the job.
  4. Once you have placed a bid, the button will change to "Bid Placed" and will be disabled.

Methods of Payment

We accept the following methods of payment:

  • Credit Card
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfer

What is Required After Bidding

After successfully bidding on a job, you are required to:

  1. Complete the assigned work within the given deadline.
  2. Submit your work using the provided file upload feature.
  3. Ensure that your submission meets the specified requirements mentioned in the job description.
  4. Once submitted, wait for confirmation or further instructions from the employer.
